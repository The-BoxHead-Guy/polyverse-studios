Our Masterpieces

Fractured Realms

In a shattered reality where fragments of different worlds collide, players embark on a journey to mend these fractures and uncover the truth behind the cataclysm.

They explore diverse landscapes using AR technology, encountering fantastical creatures and engaging in dynamic real-time battles with VR integration.

The narrative unfolds through exploration, NPC interactions, and environmental storytelling, offering different paths and endings based on player choices.

Project: Chimera

Players assume the role of genetically engineered soldiers tasked with uncovering the secrets of a hidden organization and its experiments.

They navigate sprawling urban environments and abandoned facilities, utilizing advanced weaponry and abilities like enhanced reflexes and tactical perception.

The story unfolds through missions, character interactions, and environmental clues, with moral choices impacting the narrative and potential for online co-op gameplay.

Songhaven

Players traverse a world shaped by music, manipulating the environment and solving puzzles by interacting with sound waves and musical instruments.

They explore vibrant landscapes, encounter fantastical creatures, and delve into a heartwarming narrative about harmony and self-discovery.

The gameplay revolves around manipulating musical elements, creating melodies to unlock passages, and overcoming environmental challenges.
